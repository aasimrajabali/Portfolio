1. Open up the .pde file and click 'Run'
2. To Grayscale the image, press '1'
3. To High Contrast the image, press '2'
4. To use Gaussian Blue on the image, press '3'
5. To use Edge Detection on the image, press '4'
6. To use a sepia tone on the image, press '5'
7. To invert the image, press '6'
8. To solarize the image, press '7'
9. To threshold the image, press '8'
10. To return the image back to its original, press '0'
